Domain Name Change

WE ARE NOW AN INCORPORATED ASSOCIATION Our Domain name and hosting company will soon change to telstechs.org.au
Back in the bad old days when the internet started, it was possible to name a domain anything you wanted. Since then the standards people have stepped in and changed what you can apply for. Using the word 'RAAF' in your domain is not allowed, so raaftelstechs.org.au has been put on a list of domain names that cannot be renewed. It expires in Jan 2024.

However, to get a domain name ending in .org.au we need to have an incorporated association to apply. Therefore, the committee has embarked on applying for an incorporated entity called Telstechs Association Inc.

When the Association documentation comes through, the application for the new domain name will commence.

We are also required to register with the Australian Charities and Not-for-Profit Commission (ACNC) which will give validity in the pursuit of grants for the Association.

Being a registered organisation we will be eligible to apply for grants from Commonwealth/State Governments and Corporations that support non-profit associations.

The current members and life members will be able to benefit from the proceeds of the grants as they engage with the association.

All this work is being accomplished in the background by the very small committee in an effort to benefit the members and keep the Association strong and healthy.

Stay tuned for more information regarding the changes and progress...
