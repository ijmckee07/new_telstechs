Subscriptions
Annual subscription to the Association is $10.00.
The membership year runs from 1 March to 28 February.
Life Membership is $100.

Funds are used to pay for the domain name and web site hosting services.

The domin name changed in 2023 in line with ASIC rules.
The Association was legally incorporated as TELSTECHS ASSOCIATION INC. on 17 April 2023.
This allowed the Association to register the new telstechs.org.au domain name, to be exempt from annual returns, BAS returns, ASIC registration, and costly accounting fees.
