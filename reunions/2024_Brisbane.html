Presidents Report 1 June 2024

The year, so far, has been very full. There was a Get Together held in Werribee in February which was well attended. More than 35 people. There were many from the Melbourne area, and several from interstate.

This was followed by the Annual Reunion in Brisbane in May. This was attended by about 20 people. Which was a great turnout considering so many members were touring Australia and the world. Plus, some last-minute cancellations. These cancellations were at no cost to the member or the Association.

The fewer numbers meant that attendees got more free drinks. This was boosted by a very kind gesture by Dave Middleton who donated $200 over the Bar.

A HUUUUGE thank you for that, Dave. Everyone present signed a certificate of Appreciation for Dave.
Certificate of Appreciation

Thanks to Milky and Mark Wilson, who also contributed to the event by buying some Bar Snacks.

Another benefit of there being a smaller number meant we could spend more quality time chatting with each other.

Topics that were highlighted by the President were:

• The TELSTECH Association is now incorporated.
• Due to the inability to access ADF Bases, finances are being transferred to ING.
• The Treasurer position is still vacant. (position now filled)
• There will be a 2025 Get Together in either Adelaide or Canberra. To be decided by a vote by the members.
• The 2025 Reunion will be at the Paddo on Friday, 30 May 2025.

Please ensure your contact details are current.

Stay safe and strong.

Regards

Nipper
